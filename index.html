Mufsi Gallery Privacy Policy

Last Updated: March 9, 2025

1. Data Collection

Mufsi Gallery does not collect, store, or share any personal user data. The app functions entirely offline without requiring user accounts, tracking, or data collection.

2. Permissions

The app requests:

  • READ_EXTERNAL_STORAGE – To access and display photos and videos.
  • WRITE_EXTERNAL_STORAGE – For optional image editing and saving (Android 10 and below).

3. Third-Party Services

Mufsi Gallery does not use third-party analytics, ads, or cloud storage services.

Updates will be posted at this URL. Continued use constitutes acceptance of changes.
